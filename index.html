SERVICES

NATURAL

NATURAL

The natural finish is basically the absence of treatment. The stone is sold as extracted from the quarry, once cut into the desired format.

Our Products

POLISHED

POLISHED

Stones are polished by repeated application of abrasive treatments. This technique produces a shiny surface, with almost zero porosity, while improving resistance.

Our Products

LEATHER

LEATHER

A leather finish is obtained by a process of brushing the slab with a range of brushes. The result is a slightly undulating surface, very soft, warm and smooth to the touch.
